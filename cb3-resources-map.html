About This Map

This map shows community services in Bedford-Stuyvesant and the surrounding Community Board 3 area, organized by the Northeast Brooklyn Housing Development Corporation (NEBHDCo).

Categories include:

• Health Care — Hospitals, clinics, and emergency services
• Police — NYPD precincts serving CB3
• Fire Department — FDNY engine and ladder companies
• Library — Brooklyn Public Library branches
• Food Pantry / Soup Kitchen — Emergency food resources
• Community — Civic organizations and offices
• Single Stop — Free benefits, tax, and legal services
• Senior Center — Centers serving older adults
• Community Garden — Gardens open to residents

Data source: NEBHDCo CB3 Resource Guide. Contact delaneyconnor1@gmail.com to suggest updates.
